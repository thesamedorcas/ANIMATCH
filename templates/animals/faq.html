Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up?

Click on the Signup link and fill in your details.

How do I apply for adoption?

Browse the animal listings, select an animal that interests you, and click the "Apply to Adopt" button. Make sure you are logged in to apply.

What happens after I apply for adoption?

Your application will be reviewed to ensure your profile meets the criteria for the animal.

How long does the adoption process take?

The process duration may vary. Typically, you can expect a response within a few days. In some cases, further evaluation may be required.

What if my adoption application is rejected?

If your application doesn't meet the required criteria, we will provide feedback and suggestions for improvement.

Can I update my profile after signing up?

Yes, you can update your personal details anytime in the "My Account" section.

How do I add or remove an animal listing?

Once logged in, navigate to your account to manage your animal listings. You can add new listings or remove existing ones as needed.

Who can see my animal listing?

All public animal listings are visible to anyone browsing the site. However, only logged in users can apply for adoption.
